Welcome to Sisi Estate

Your Most Trusted Property Partner in Arusha

Sisi Estate Company Limited is a premiere land development and estates firm specializing in surveyed land distribution, civil engineering, and legal ownership transfer.

With years of local presence, we eliminate the traditional risks associated with buying land in Tanzania. Every single plot in our portfolio has undergone strict layout verification, physical beacon placement, and registry validation.
